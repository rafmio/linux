HI - human interfaces
HID - human interface devices (устройство взаимодействи с человеком)
The term "HID" most commonly refers to the USB HID specification.

In the HID protocol there are 2 entities:
- host
- device

User peripherals:
- keyboard
- camera
- mouse 
- graphics card
- audio

HI peripherals device drivers
